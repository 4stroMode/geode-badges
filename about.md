# Badges For Players

This mod adds custom badges to prominent players in the Geometry Dash community to give them more visibility and prestige. 

We are adding badges for **<co>Creators</c>**, **<cg>Players</c>**, and **Social Media** icons. Social media badges have 3 different levels depending on your prominence!

<cy>Available Badges:</c>
* **Owner**
* **YouTube** (Levels 1, 2, 3)
* **Twitch** (Levels 1, 2, 3)
* **X / Twitter** (Levels 1, 2, 3)
* **Creator**
* **Player**
